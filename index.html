Who We Are

Wellness rooted in science,
guided by heart

Fit & Shine Wellness Centre was founded by husband-and-wife team Kartik and Shilpa with one belief: every person deserves expert guidance on their wellness journey. For 15+ years we've been transforming lives in Ahmedabad — combining advanced treatments with personalised nutrition science and compassionate coaching.

We address root causes — hormonal imbalances, poor nutrition, metabolic issues — to create sustainable wellness that truly lasts.

Co-Founder & Certified Wellness Coach

Coach Kartik

With 15+ years of hands-on experience transforming lives across Ahmedabad, Coach Kartik is your partner in achieving real, lasting fitness results. A certified wellness expert with a passion for human potential, he has guided thousands of clients through weight loss, muscle building, sports nutrition, and lifestyle disease reversal — all through personalised, science-backed programs.

His approach goes beyond the scale — he coaches the whole person: mindset, movement, and metabolic health. Whether you're a student, athlete, new parent, or senior, Coach Kartik designs a plan that fits your life — not the other way around.

Weight Loss Sports Nutrition Lifestyle Diseases Fitness Coaching
Co-Founder & Certified Nutrition Specialist

Coach Shilpa

Coach Shilpa is the nutrition heart of Fit & Shine — a certified dietitian and wellness coach who believes that the right food, at the right time, in the right quantity, can heal almost anything. Her meal plans are rooted in the latest nutritional science while remaining deeply practical, culturally relevant, and easy to follow in everyday Indian households.

From weight management to child nutrition, skin health, joint support, and post-pregnancy recovery — Coach Shilpa crafts plans that produce visible results and become sustainable habits for life.
