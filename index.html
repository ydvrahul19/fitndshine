Who We Are

Wellness rooted in science,
guided by heart

Fit & Shine Wellness Centre was founded by husband-and-wife team Kartik and Shilpa with one belief: every person deserves expert guidance on their wellness journey. For 15+ years we've been transforming lives in Ahmedabad — combining advanced treatments with personalised nutrition science and compassionate coaching.

We address root causes — hormonal imbalances, poor nutrition, metabolic issues — to create sustainable wellness that truly lasts.

Co-Founder & Wellness Expert

Coach Kartik & Shilpa

With 15+ years in fitness and wellness, Kartik specialises in transformative weight loss. His holistic, results-driven approach has helped thousands of clients across Ahmedabad rebuild their confidence and reclaim their health.

Co-Founder & Nutrition Specialist

Shilpa

Shilpa is a certified nutritionist and wellness coach specialising in sustainable diet planning. Her meal plans are science-backed, culturally aware, easy to follow, and deeply effective at accelerating transformation.
